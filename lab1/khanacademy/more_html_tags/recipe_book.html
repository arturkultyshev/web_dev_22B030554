Borscht

  • Time: 4 h 30 m
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
beef 300 g
potatoes 500 g
white cabbage 400 g
onion 2 onion
garlic 3 cloves
carrots 100 g
beet 300 g
clarified butter 2 tsp
sour cream 150 g

Step 1: Boil the meat in 3 liters of water, add salt and pepper. Beets, cabbage and onions cut into strips, potatoes - slices.

Step 2: Beet fry in butter, add a tablespoon of vinegar (3%), stew until tender in small amount of broth, add sugar (1 tablespoon), tomato paste (2 tablespoons).

Step 3: Strain the broth and continue to cook. Meat cut into cubes and put in boiling broth, add potatoes, cabbage and cook for 15 minutes.

Step 4: Then add the beets, vegetables and sweet pepper cut in shreds, spices. Cook for another 15 minutes.

Step 5: Add chopped garlic, pounded with fat and bring to a boil. Then let stand another 20 minutes.
